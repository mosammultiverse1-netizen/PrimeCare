About PrimeCare Revenue Services

We are a global healthcare revenue cycle management company dedicated to optimizing financial performance for healthcare providers worldwide.

PrimeCare Revenue Services specializes in comprehensive revenue cycle management solutions for the healthcare industry with offices in Pakistan and USA. Our team of certified professionals brings extensive experience in medical billing, coding, and healthcare administration.

We combine cutting-edge technology with industry expertise to streamline operations, reduce claim denials, and maximize revenue for our clients across the globe.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare providers worldwide with efficient revenue cycle solutions that improve cash flow and allow them to focus on patient care.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted global revenue cycle partner for healthcare providers across continents.

Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare revenue solutions tailored to your practice's needs.

Revenue Cycle Management

End-to-end RCM services including patient registration, coding, billing, payment posting, and denial management.

Medical Billing

Accurate medical billing for physicians, hospitals, clinics, and outpatient facilities.

DME Billing

Specialized billing for Durable Medical Equipment providers with insurance verification and prior authorization.

Claim Denial Management

Identify, appeal, and reduce claim denials to maximize revenue recovery.

Eligibility Verification

Real-time insurance eligibility verification to reduce claim rejections.

Data Management

Secure, HIPAA-compliant data entry and EMR/EHR management services.
